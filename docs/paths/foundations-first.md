# Foundations First

!!! tip "Who this is for"
    Brand-new players, or anyone building the defensive shell and range sense the rest of the system assumes. **This is the free starter path.**

**The journey:** Build a reliable defense one solution at a time, integrate those solutions under pressure, then learn to manage range and initiative — the on-ramp to everything else.

## The Path

1. **[Tight Block](../games/tight-block.md)** — Build the blocking shell and composure under fire, absorbing punches behind a tight guard.
2. **[Parry the Straight](../games/parry-the-straight.md)** — Develop the parry as a dedicated skill: deflecting straights through pattern recognition and timing.
3. **[Slip the Straight](../games/slip-the-straight.md)** — Develop the slip as a dedicated evasion skill: moving the head offline to make straights miss.
4. **[Evade the Punch](../games/evade-the-punch.md)** — Integrate evasions by matching the right head movement to the punch type — slip straights, duck hooks.
5. **[Close-Range Defense](../games/close-range-defense.md)** — Build defensive adaptability: choosing when to block, parry, or dodge as solutions scale up.
6. **[Touch Game](../games/touch-game.md)** — Manage initiative and range/timing — the foundation before space, pressure, and clinch.

## Prerequisites

None — this is where you start.

## Where this leads

Once your defense and range feel automatic, branch into **[Striking Offense](striking-offense.md)** to build your attack, or **[Closing the Distance](closing-the-distance.md)** to turn range control into clinch and takedown entries.
