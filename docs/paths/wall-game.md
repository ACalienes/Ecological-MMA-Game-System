# The Wall Game

!!! tip "Who this is for"
    Players who can pressure an opponent to the cage and want to own the vertical battle, pinning, grinding, escaping, and cycling the wall.

**The journey:** Arrive at the wall, establish a dominant pin, exploit it for damage, defend it, and learn how wall positions cycle rather than resolve.

## The Path

1. **[Pressure to Wall](../games/pressure-to-wall.md)**, Chain strikes into a controlling, damaging wall position for wall dominance.
2. **[Wall Control](../games/wall-control.md)**, Use the wall as the dominant constraint to organize vertical stabilization and control.
3. **[Wall Grinding](../games/wall-grinding.md)**, Deal damage from the wall while keeping control, distinct from transitioning to ground.
4. **[Wall Escape](../games/wall-escape.md)**, Escape and reverse off the wall, the defensive answer to wall control.
5. **[Wall to Ground](../games/wall-to-ground.md)**, Make the controlled transition from wall to ground, where control precedes gravity.
6. **[Stand-Up Loop](../games/standup-loop.md)**, Manage the loop: understand how wall positions cycle rather than resolve in one action.

## Prerequisites

Complete **[Closing the Distance](closing-the-distance.md)**, both Pressure to Wall and Wall Control require [Pressure to Clinch](../games/pressure-to-clinch.md), and Wall Control gates every later step here.

## Where this leads

Wall to Ground and the Stand-Up Loop both spill into **[The Ground Game](ground-game.md)**, and the full wall sequence is a core thread of **[The Complete Fight](complete-fight.md)**.
