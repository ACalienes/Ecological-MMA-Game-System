# Closing the Distance

!!! tip "Who this is for"
    Players comfortable in open space who want to stop trading at range and start dictating where the fight happens — the clinch, the wall, or the mat.

**The journey:** Win the circle, convert spatial control into a clinch, and chain that pressure into wall pins and takedowns.

## The Path

1. **[Touch Game](../games/touch-game.md)** — Manage initiative and range/timing — the foundation before space, pressure, and clinch.
2. **[Pressure to Clinch](../games/pressure-to-clinch.md)** — Win the circle: manage distance and convert spatial control into the clinch.
3. **[Pressure to Wall](../games/pressure-to-wall.md)** — Chain strikes into a controlling, damaging wall position for wall dominance.
4. **[Pressure to Takedown](../games/pressure-to-takedown.md)** — Chain strikes to a takedown while managing all three threats at once on defense.
5. **[Open Space Takedown](../games/open-space-takedown.md)** — Land controlled takedowns where escape routes and DNS threats are at their most dangerous.

## Prerequisites

Complete **[Foundations First](foundations-first.md)** — Touch Game starts this path, and Pressure to Takedown also requires [Close-Range Defense](../games/close-range-defense.md). On the defensive side, pair this path with [Takedown Defense](../games/takedown-defense.md) and [Counter-Wrestling](../games/counter-wrestling.md), the counters to the entries you're drilling here.

## Where this leads

Wall entries open into **[The Wall Game](wall-game.md)**; takedowns open into **[The Ground Game](ground-game.md)**. Both converge in **[The Complete Fight](complete-fight.md)**.
