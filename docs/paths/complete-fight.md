# The Complete Fight

!!! tip "Who this is for"
    Advanced players who have worked the individual paths and want to thread every phase together — from first touch at range to a finish on the mat.

**The journey:** Run one continuous arc through all four phases — striking range, clinch, wall, and ground — chaining each transition toward a TKO or submission.

## The Path

1. **[Touch Game](../games/touch-game.md)** — Manage initiative and range/timing — the opening phase, controlling distance.
2. **[Pressure to Clinch](../games/pressure-to-clinch.md)** — Win the circle: convert spatial control into the clinch.
3. **[Pressure to Takedown](../games/pressure-to-takedown.md)** — Chain strikes to a takedown while managing all three threats on defense.
4. **[Wall Control](../games/wall-control.md)** — Use the wall as the dominant constraint to organize control.
5. **[Wall to Ground](../games/wall-to-ground.md)** — Make the controlled transition from wall to ground — control precedes gravity.
6. **[Ground Control](../games/ground-control.md)** — Train the Stabilize stage: control that holds while creating openings to finish.
7. **The finish** — Convert control into the end state: a TKO from accumulated damage or a submission from a stabilized position.

## Prerequisites

This is the capstone — complete every prior path first: **[Foundations First](foundations-first.md)**, **[Striking Offense](striking-offense.md)**, **[Closing the Distance](closing-the-distance.md)**, **[The Wall Game](wall-game.md)**, and **[The Ground Game](ground-game.md)**. In particular, Ground Control sits at the end of the Ground Game's own chain (Ground Escape → Positional Battle → Ground Access → Ground Control), so that path must be complete before the finish here makes sense.

## Where this leads

This is the end of the journey — the point where the isolated skills become one fight. From here, raise the level inside any game toward full MMA expression.
