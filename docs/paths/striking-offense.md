# Striking Offense

!!! tip "Who this is for"
    Players who can defend and manage range, and now want to build an attack, from isolating single weapons to sustaining pressure toward a finish.

**The journey:** Sharpen each striking weapon in isolation, then combine them into combinations that land, and finally sustain offense through resistance.

## The Path

1. **[Lead Hand Offense](../games/lead-hand-offense.md)**, Develop the lead hand as a weapon, finding variety and timing within a tight constraint.
2. **[Rear Hand Offense](../games/rear-hand-offense.md)**, Develop the rear (power) hand, finding timing, entries, and setups for power shots.
3. **[Lead Leg Offense](../games/lead-leg-offense.md)**, Develop the lead leg, finding range, timing, and variety within a tight constraint.
4. **[Rear Leg Offense](../games/rear-leg-offense.md)**, Develop the rear (power) leg, finding timing, setups, and entries for power kicks.
5. **[Land the Target](../games/land-the-target.md)**, Build target selection, combination construction, and misdirection without a counter-threat.
6. **[Sustained Offense](../games/sustained-offense.md)**, Develop sustained pressure: maintaining the attack through resistance rather than single-shot landing.

## Prerequisites

Complete **[Foundations First](foundations-first.md)** to defend and manage range before you commit to attacking. Land the Target and Sustained Offense also assume the [Three Zones](../concepts/three-zones.md) and [Confidence Rating](../concepts/confidence-rating.md) concepts.

## Where this leads

Sustained Offense feeds the KO/TKO finish in **[The Complete Fight](complete-fight.md)**. To turn forward pressure into grappling, move to **[Closing the Distance](closing-the-distance.md)**.
