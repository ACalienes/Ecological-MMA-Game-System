# Ecological Psychology

<div class="emma-meta"><span>Theory Layer</span><span>The Science Underneath</span><span>Blau &amp; Wagman (2022)</span></div>

The science our whole training system rests on. Where the [Constraints-Led Approach](../cla/index.md) is the *method* we coach with, ecological psychology is the *science* underneath it, the lawful account of how animals perceive, act, and learn.

<p class="emma-glabel">The One Idea</p>

<p class="emma-hl"><b>The animal and its environment are one system.</b> You cannot understand the fighter without the cage and the opponent, they are part of the system from the start, not "factors" added on. And the fighter does not compute the world from blurry input; the environment <b>lawfully structures energy</b> (light, sound, force) and the fighter <b>directly detects</b> it. Nothing is processed, only picked up. <i>(Blau &amp; Wagman, 2022, p. 56–57, 82)</i></p>

These two moves, **animal–environment mutuality** and **direct perception**, are the foundation. Everything else (affordances, synergies, attractors, self-organization, learning) follows from them.

<p class="emma-glabel">Two Pillars</p>

<div class="emma-cards">
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🔗</div><div class="emma-card__t">Mutuality</div><p>The unit of analysis is the <b>fighter–opponent–cage system</b>, never the fighter alone. Fred's fear on a rickety bridge lives in the Fred-bridge system because he cannot fly, his action capabilities define the meaning of the event. <i>(p. 57)</i></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">👁️</div><div class="emma-card__t">Direct Perception</div><p>No central executive "fixes" ambiguous input. The animal detects lawfully structured information directly. Perception through a tool (a grip, a glove) is still direct, directness is about not being mediated by anything <i>mental</i>. <i>(p. 56, 82, 219)</i></p></div>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">The 14 Principles</p>

<p>The core ideas, and what each means in the cage. Each links to its full write-up on <a href="principles/">The Principles</a>.</p>

<div class="emma-cards">
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🔗</div><div class="emma-card__t"><a href="principles/#mutuality-ecological-scale">Mutuality &amp; Scale</a></div><p>The fighter–opponent–cage system is the unit. Describe behavior at the macroscopic scale, never the joint-angle scale.</p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">👁️</div><div class="emma-card__t"><a href="principles/#direct-perception">Direct Perception</a></div><p>Detect, don't compute. Train attention to information, never an internal calculation.</p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🌊</div><div class="emma-card__t"><a href="principles/#information-invariants">Information &amp; Invariants</a></div><p>Attune to higher-order invariants, τ, optic flow, the inertial array, not raw data.</p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🎯</div><div class="emma-card__t"><a href="principles/#affordances">Affordances</a></div><p>Opportunities for action in the animal–world relationship. Reward the <i>taken</i> opportunity.</p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">📏</div><div class="emma-card__t"><a href="principles/#body-scaling-critical-ratios">Body-Scaling (π)</a></div><p>Affordances scale as a ratio. Set range/height by reach and leg length, not absolutes.</p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">♾️</div><div class="emma-card__t"><a href="principles/#perceptionaction-loop">Perception–Action Loop</a></div><p>One continuous loop, never two stages. Never decompose it.</p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🎼</div><div class="emma-card__t"><a href="principles/#synergies-degrees-of-freedom">Synergies &amp; DoF</a></div><p>The body soft-assembles "choirs of muscles" that preserve the goal, not fixed parts.</p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🪶</div><div class="emma-card__t"><a href="principles/#forces-for-free">Forces for Free</a></div><p>Cooperate with gravity and reactive forces, "walking is controlled falling."</p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🔁</div><div class="emma-card__t"><a href="principles/#repetition-without-repetition">Repetition w/o Repetition</a></div><p>Repeat the <i>problem</i>, not the movement. Variability is the feature.</p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🏔️</div><div class="emma-card__t"><a href="principles/#dynamical-systems-attractors">Dynamical Systems</a></div><p>Skill is an attractor layout the coach reshapes by turning control parameters.</p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🐜</div><div class="emma-card__t"><a href="principles/#self-organization">Self-Organization</a></div><p>Order emerges from local rules + interaction. The coach is a rule-giver, not a choreographer.</p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">📚</div><div class="emma-card__t"><a href="principles/#learning-attunement">Learning = Attunement</a></div><p>Educate intention → educate attention → calibrate the response.</p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🚧</div><div class="emma-card__t"><a href="principles/#no-transfer-across-modes">No Transfer Across Modes</a></div><p>Reading τ for striking ≠ for takedowns. Build separate per-mode paths.</p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🤝</div><div class="emma-card__t"><a href="principles/#perceiving-acting-with-others">Perceiving With Others</a></div><p>Perceive affordances for self AND opponent. Kendo's two preferred distances.</p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">✋</div><div class="emma-card__t"><a href="principles/#dynamic-touch-haptics">Dynamic Touch</a></div><p>Feel structure and off-balance-ability through the inertial array at the grip.</p></div>
</div>
