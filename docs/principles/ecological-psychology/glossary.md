# Glossary

<div class="emma-meta"><span>Theory Layer</span><span>Key Terms</span><span>Crisp Definitions</span></div>

The vocabulary of ecological psychology, defined crisply. For the full treatment of each, see [The Principles](principles.md); for the studies, [The Evidence](landmark-studies.md). This complements the methodology-focused [CLA glossary](../cla/glossary.md).

<div class="emma-cards">
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🎯</div><div class="emma-card__t">Affordance</div><p>An opportunity for action that emerges from the relationship between animal and environment, neither in the world nor in the animal, but between them. Often phrased with "-able." <i>(p. 73–78)</i></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🌊</div><div class="emma-card__t">Invariant</div><p>A higher-order pattern in structured energy that stays constant across transformations and lawfully <b>specifies</b> an affordance. <i>(p. 82)</i></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">📡</div><div class="emma-card__t">Specification</div><p>The lawful, unambiguous relationship by which an energy pattern carries information <i>about</i> an affordance, so it can be detected, not inferred. <i>(p. 82)</i></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">👁️</div><div class="emma-card__t">Direct perception</div><p>Detecting lawfully structured information without a mental intermediary (no representation, no inference, no central executive). Tools may mediate physically; perception stays direct. <i>(p. 56, 82, 219)</i></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">⏱️</div><div class="emma-card__t">Tau (τ)</div><p>The relative rate of optical expansion of a texture; specifies <b>time-to-contact / time-to-arrival</b> assuming current conditions continue. The information for timing. <i>(p. 74, 165–166)</i></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🎼</div><div class="emma-card__t">Synergy</div><p>A coordinative structure, a temporary "choir" of potentially independent components soft-assembled to a task; goal-preserving and self-correcting. Bernstein's solution to the degrees-of-freedom problem. <i>(p. 144–146)</i></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🏔️</div><div class="emma-card__t">Attractor</div><p>A stable behavior the system falls into and that attracts nearby behaviors "like a magnet attracts iron." The deepest attractor is the most stable and usually the most efficient. <i>(p. 234–238)</i></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🎛️</div><div class="emma-card__t">Control parameter</div><p>A variable that, when changed, reorganizes the attractor layout (speed, pressure, range). It changes the layout, it does not directly dictate the new behavior. <i>(p. 250)</i></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">📍</div><div class="emma-card__t">Order parameter</div><p>The collective / macro variable that captures the coordination state, e.g., relative phase between two oscillators. What you measure. <i>(p. 251)</i></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🐜</div><div class="emma-card__t">Self-organization</div><p>Pattern emerging from minimally intelligent components following <b>local rules</b> with constant mutual interaction, no leader, no blueprint, comprehensible only at a larger scale. <i>(p. 270–272)</i></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🔎</div><div class="emma-card__t">Education of attention</div><p>Learning which components of a structured energy array carry the most useful information for an intended affordance, and shifting toward them. <i>(p. 204–205)</i></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🎚️</div><div class="emma-card__t">Calibration</div><p>Learning how to scale perception/action to the detected information, the information-to-action mapping. Specific to the action mode; does <b>not</b> transfer across modes. <i>(p. 206)</i></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">📏</div><div class="emma-card__t">Critical π ratio</div><p>The body-scaled value at which an affordance appears or disappears (e.g., step ÷ leg = 0.88 for stepping). How "individualize" becomes precise. <i>(Warren, p. 79–81)</i></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">♻️</div><div class="emma-card__t">Degeneracy / multirealizability</div><p>Many different solutions reaching the same goal (and the same parts reorganizing for many goals). Robustness through variety. <i>(p. 274)</i></p></div>
</div>
