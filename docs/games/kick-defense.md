    Built on the mat on 2026-06-20 to isolate the kick-defense read, the first dedicated kick-defense game in the library. It trains checking, blocking, and catching the roundhouse, one kick at a time, then layers a jab to make the kicker tricky. Awaiting a final pass before the WIP tag comes off.

<div class="emma-meta"><span>Skill Isolation</span><span>Striking</span><span>Defensive</span><span>Intermediate</span><span>Kick Defense</span></div>

Read the kick and kill it, check, block, or catch, before it lands, one kick at a time.

<div class="emma-arc">
  <div class="emma-arc__node"><span class="emma-arc__k">Start</span><b>One kicker, one defender, inside a marked perimeter; roundhouses only, one at a time.</b></div>
  <div class="emma-arc__sep">→</div>
  <div class="emma-arc__node"><span class="emma-arc__k">The Goal</span><b>Defender checks, blocks, or catches the kick; kicker lands clean and earns the next one.</b></div>
  <div class="emma-arc__sep">→</div>
  <div class="emma-arc__node emma-arc__node--finish"><span class="emma-arc__k">Finish</span><b>A clean defense → roles switch. A clean landed kick → kicker continues. Catch and keep → gold star.</b></div>
</div>

<div class="emma-band">
  <p class="emma-statement">You can't block what you read late. <br><span class="accent">Time the kick, don't chase it.</span></p>
  <p class="emma-statement__sub">Attune to the rising shin and the hip that throws it, the time-to-contact, not the foot. <b>Catch only when it's really there, reach for everything and you get faked to the head.</b></p>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">What to Read</p>

<p class="emma-hl"><b>Attune to</b> <i>τ (time-to-contact)</i>, the closing rate of the rising shin, and the <i>hip and shoulder pre-rotation</i> that says which kick is coming and where. Defend on the read, not the impact. The <i>catch</i> is a higher-risk, higher-reward read than the block, take it only when τ gives you the room.</p>

<p class="emma-glabel">The Starting Position</p>

<div class="emma-setup">
  <div class="emma-setup__item"><span class="emma-setup__k">Players</span>Two, one kicker (offense), one defender.</div>
  <div class="emma-setup__item"><span class="emma-setup__k">Position</span>Striking range, both on their feet, roundhouses only. Switch stance freely.</div>
  <div class="emma-setup__item"><span class="emma-setup__k">Boundary</span>A marked perimeter, the wall is live, use it to corner the kicker or get walked onto it yourself.</div>
  <div class="emma-setup__item"><span class="emma-setup__k">Roles</span>Kicker lands clean roundhouses one at a time; defender checks, blocks, or catches.</div>
  <div class="emma-setup__item"><span class="emma-setup__k">Start &amp; reset</span>"Go"; roles switch the moment the defender successfully checks, blocks, or catches. A clean, undefended kick earns the kicker the next one.</div>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">The Matchup</p>

<div class="emma-matchup">
  <div class="emma-fighter emma-fighter--atk emma-has-pop" tabindex="0">
    <div class="emma-fighter__icon">🦵</div>
    <div class="emma-fighter__role">Kicker (Landing)</div>
    <p class="emma-fighter__obj">Trying to land a clean roundhouse to the leg or body, one at a time, and earn the right to throw the next one.</p>
    <span class="emma-pop">One kick at a time, no spamming. Use feints and stance switches to disguise the kick, and gauge your own commitment, a heavy kick that gets checked hurts you. In the jab variant, use a lead jab to open the kick. Don't leave a caught leg hanging, pull it free.</span>
  </div>
  <div class="emma-matchup__vs">VS</div>
  <div class="emma-fighter emma-fighter--def emma-has-pop" tabindex="0">
    <div class="emma-fighter__icon">🛡️</div>
    <div class="emma-fighter__role">Defender (Reading)</div>
    <p class="emma-fighter__obj">Trying to check, block, or catch the kick on the early read, and take over the offense.</p>
    <span class="emma-pop">Load your weight on the back leg, ready to check. Read τ and the hip, not the foot. Check with the shin, block with two points of contact, and catch only when the time is really there. Keep your fingers curled. In the later levels, return fire the moment you defend.</span>
  </div>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">The Rules</p>

<div class="emma-chips">
  <span class="emma-chip emma-chip--info" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-chip__i">🦵</span> Roundhouses only, one at a time<span class="emma-chip__detail">Leg or body roundhouses; head only with full control. No spamming, throw one, read the result, then throw again. The kicker continues only by landing clean.</span></span>
  <span class="emma-chip emma-chip--info" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-chip__i">🛡️</span> Check, block, or catch wins the exchange<span class="emma-chip__detail">Check = the shin, above the ankle and below the knee. Block = two points of contact absorbing the kick. Catch = secure and keep the leg, the gold star. Any of the three switches the offense to the defender.</span></span>
  <span class="emma-chip emma-chip--info" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-chip__i">🔁</span> Land clean and continue<span class="emma-chip__detail">A clean, undefended kick earns the kicker the next one. A successful defense switches the roles, the defender becomes the kicker.</span></span>
  <span class="emma-chip emma-chip--info" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-chip__i">👊</span> Jab variant<span class="emma-chip__detail">The kicker may add a lead jab as a probe. It scores no points, it only opens the kick. The defender may return fire or counter for a bonus.</span></span>
  <span class="emma-chip emma-chip--info" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-chip__i">🚫</span> No head kicks, no calf kicks<span class="emma-chip__detail">The coordination-stage nerf, it makes the catch safe to learn. The mastery level puts the head-kick threat back, with gear, so the catch carries its real risk.</span></span>
  <span class="emma-chip emma-chip--info" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-chip__i">✋</span> Curl the fingers<span class="emma-chip__detail">Open, splayed fingers get jammed and broken, and they foul. Keep a soft curl on every block and catch. A real safety habit, learned here so it holds in a fight.</span></span>
  <span class="emma-chip emma-chip--info" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-chip__i">⬛</span> Stay inside the perimeter<span class="emma-chip__detail">Play happens inside a marked perimeter. Leaving it loses the exchange.</span></span>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">How to Win</p>

<div class="emma-outcomes">
  <div class="emma-outcome emma-outcome--win emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-outcome__tag">Defender</span> Check, block, or catch the kick → win the exchange and take the offense.<span class="emma-pop">Any clean defense wins the exchange and hands you the offense. Catch and keep the leg for the gold star, and a returned counter on top of the defense is the full expression.</span></div>
  <div class="emma-outcome emma-outcome--win emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-outcome__tag">Kicker</span> Land a clean, undefended roundhouse → continue and earn the next kick.<span class="emma-pop">A clean kick to the leg or body that is not checked, blocked, or caught keeps you on offense. One at a time, read each result before the next.</span></div>
  <div class="emma-outcome emma-outcome--lose emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-outcome__tag">Switch / loss</span> A defended kick hands over the offense; a caught and kept leg can be punished.<span class="emma-pop">Getting checked, blocked, or caught switches the roles. A leg left hanging in a catch can be swept or countered, so pull it free.</span></div>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">The Levels</p>

<div class="emma-stepper">
  <div class="emma-step emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-step__n">1</span><b>Check and block</b>Read τ, no jab.<span class="emma-pop emma-pop--l">Roundhouses only, no jab, no head kicks. The defender reads τ and the hip, and checks or blocks. Finds the early read.</span></div>
  <div class="emma-step emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-step__n">2</span><b>Add the catch</b>The gold star, but risky.<span class="emma-pop emma-pop--l">Catching and keeping the leg becomes a win. But don't reach for everything, an over-reach against a tricky kicker gets faked. Take the catch only when τ affords it.</span></div>
  <div class="emma-step emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-step__n">3</span><b>Add the jab</b>The kicker gets tricky.<span class="emma-pop emma-pop--l">The kicker adds a lead jab to probe and open the kick. The defender has to read through the misdirection.</span></div>
  <div class="emma-step emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-step__n">4</span><b>Return fire</b>Defend and punish.<span class="emma-pop emma-pop--l">A defense plus an immediate counter is the full win. Check and return, or catch and sweep, the defender stops being a wall and starts punishing.</span></div>
  <div class="emma-step emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-step__n">5</span><b>Mastery: the head kick returns</b>Calibrate the real risk.<span class="emma-pop emma-pop--l">With control and gear, head kicks are live again. Now the catch carries its real risk and the read has to be honest, this calibrates the safe habit from the earlier levels to a real fight.</span></div>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">Recall Check</p>

<div class="emma-recall">
  <p class="emma-recall__lead">Test yourself before moving on. Answer out loud, then reveal what good looks like.</p>
  <details class="emma-recall__item">
    <summary><span class="emma-recall__q">Q</span> What are you actually reading, the foot or something earlier?</summary>
    <div class="emma-recall__a"><b>τ, the time-to-contact</b> of the rising shin, and the <b>hip and shoulder</b> that throw it. Defend on the early read, not at the impact, by the time you see the foot it is too late.</div>
  </details>
  <details class="emma-recall__item">
    <summary><span class="emma-recall__q">Q</span> Why not reach to catch every kick?</summary>
    <div class="emma-recall__a">The catch is a <b>high-risk read</b>. Reach for everything and a good kicker <b>feints and goes to the head</b>. Catch only when τ gives you the room, otherwise block safely.</div>
  </details>
  <details class="emma-recall__item">
    <summary><span class="emma-recall__q">Q</span> Why does the no-head-kick rule come off at the mastery level?</summary>
    <div class="emma-recall__a">Nerfing the head kick makes the catch <b>artificially safe</b>. To calibrate the catch's real risk you have to put the head-kick threat back, or the safe habit <b>won't hold</b> in a real fight.</div>
  </details>
  <details class="emma-recall__item">
    <summary><span class="emma-recall__q">Q</span> Did your kick defense carry over from your grappling or boxing?</summary>
    <div class="emma-recall__a">No. Attunement <b>does not transfer across action modes</b>, reading a kick is its own skill, learned from your own stance. That is exactly why this game <b>isolates</b> it instead of assuming it comes free from another base.</div>
  </details>
</div>

    <p class="emma-hl">Implements <b>per-mode learning</b> (the non-transfer rule): kick-defense attunement does not carry over from a grappling or boxing base, so it gets its own isolated path. The kicker stays live and adaptive (feints, stance switches), so this is a <b>task-simplified</b> game with the perception-action loop intact, not a decomposed feed.</p>

    <p class="emma-sub">What athletes read</p>

    <div class="emma-cards">
      <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">👁️</div><div class="emma-card__t">Visual</div><p>τ of the rising shin and the hip / shoulder pre-rotation → which kick, where, and when, the early read that beats the impact.</p></div>
      <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">✋</div><div class="emma-card__t">Haptic</div><p>The kick's weight on the block, the leg in the catch → how committed the kick was, whether the catch will hold.</p></div>
      <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🧭</div><div class="emma-card__t">Proprioceptive</div><p>Own stance loaded on the back leg → whether you are positioned to check, or stuck flat and late.</p></div>
    </div>

    <p class="emma-sub">What we measure (order parameter)</p>

    <p class="emma-hl">Whether the defender <b>defends on the early τ read</b>, checking and blocking in time and catching only when afforded, versus reacting at impact and reaching for catches. Track defenses on the read versus late reactions, and faked-catch losses. When the defender reliably reads the hip and the closing shin, the kick-defense skill has formed.</p>

    <p class="emma-sub">Representativeness</p>

    <p class="emma-hl"><b>Models:</b> the live kick-defense problem in striking and MMA, the check / block / catch decision under feints. A per-mode skill that does not transfer in from grappling, so it earns its own isolated game.</p>

      <li>Catches only when τ affords it, doesn't reach</li>
      <li>Keeps a curled hand on every block and catch</li>
      <li>Stays composed under feints and stance switches</li>
