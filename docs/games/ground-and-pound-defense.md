<div class="emma-meta"><span>Ground</span><span>Mixed</span><span>Defensive</span><span>Advanced</span><span>WIP</span></div>

Escape the open-camped top before the damage stacks up.

<div class="emma-arc">
  <div class="emma-arc__node"><span class="emma-arc__k">Start</span><b>Bottom supine; top past the hips but NOT closed in, off to the side, no chest-to-chest, no knee-on-belly.</b></div>
  <div class="emma-arc__sep">→</div>
  <div class="emma-arc__node"><span class="emma-arc__k">The Goal</span><b>Bottom stands clean, reverses, or closes a leg loop on head-and-arm; top stays open-camped and lands ground-and-pound.</b></div>
  <div class="emma-arc__sep">→</div>
  <div class="emma-arc__node emma-arc__node--finish"><span class="emma-arc__k">Finish</span><b>Stand clean, reverse, or closed loop → bottom · Open-camped 20s, no escape → reset · Top closes the position → reset (rule break) · Out of bounds → loss.</b></div>
</div>

<div class="emma-band">
  <p class="emma-statement">Top can't pin, <br><span class="accent">so bottom has to move.</span></p>
  <p class="emma-statement__sub">Three observable escapes: stand, reverse, closed loop. <b>Closing the loop is enough, the finish is post-game.</b></p>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">Key Positions</p>

<div class="emma-chips">
  <span class="emma-chip emma-chip--info" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-chip__i">🔓</span> Open camp<span class="emma-chip__detail">Top past the hips but NOT closed in, no chest-to-chest, no knee-on-belly, no body lock. Top stays off to the side, leaving the space open as the defining constraint of the game.</span></span>
  <span class="emma-chip emma-chip--info" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-chip__i">🪢</span> Closed loop<span class="emma-chip__detail">Bottom's legs closed around top's head and arm, the structural geometry of triangle/armbar entries. The closed loop wins regardless of submission progress, the finish is post-game.</span></span>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">What to Read</p>

<p class="emma-hl"><b>Attune to</b> the <i>load shifts in the open camp</i>, the moment top's hips drop to land a heavier shot, the hand that commits to a strike and leaves an arm or head reachable. That weight shift specifies <i>which escape is open</i>, the lightened side stands, the committed arm closes the loop. Read weight, not eyes.</p>

<p class="emma-glabel">The Starting Position</p>

<div class="emma-setup">
  <div class="emma-setup__item"><span class="emma-setup__k">Players</span>Two, one bottom (defender, the protagonist), one top (open-camped).</div>
  <div class="emma-setup__item"><span class="emma-setup__k">Position</span>Bottom supine, hips on the mat. Top kneeling or posting past the hips, off to the side, past the line of the hips but not past the line of the knees. No chest-to-chest, no knee-on-belly.</div>
  <div class="emma-setup__item"><span class="emma-setup__k">Boundary</span>A marked perimeter on mats, both stay inside.</div>
  <div class="emma-setup__item"><span class="emma-setup__k">Roles</span>Bottom escapes by stand, reverse, or closed loop. Top stays open-camped and lands ground-and-pound, top cannot win.</div>
  <div class="emma-setup__item"><span class="emma-setup__k">Start &amp; reset</span>Begin from open camp; reset on any bottom escape, the 20-second cap, a rule break, or out of bounds.</div>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">The Matchup</p>

<div class="emma-matchup">
  <div class="emma-fighter emma-fighter--def emma-has-pop" tabindex="0">
    <div class="emma-fighter__icon">🤸</div>
    <div class="emma-fighter__role">Bottom (Defender)</div>
    <p class="emma-fighter__obj">Trying to stand clean, reverse to top, or close a leg loop around top's head and arm.</p>
    <span class="emma-pop">Three observable terminal escapes. Stand-clean requires full separation, if top connects on a leg or hip on the way up, not over yet. Reverse means ending on top. Closed loop is the structural geometry of triangle or armbar around head-and-arm, the finish is post-game.</span>
  </div>
  <div class="emma-matchup__vs">VS</div>
  <div class="emma-fighter emma-fighter--atk emma-has-pop" tabindex="0">
    <div class="emma-fighter__icon">🥊</div>
    <div class="emma-fighter__role">Top (Open Camp)</div>
    <p class="emma-fighter__obj">Trying to stay open-camped past the hips and land significant ground-and-pound.</p>
    <span class="emma-pop">No chest-to-chest, no knee-on-belly, no body lock. Stay off to the side, past the hips not past the knees. Damage is the only output, top cannot win the game, the constraint isolates the open-top ground-and-pound problem rather than letting it collapse into a regular pin.</span>
  </div>

<p class="emma-glabel">The Rules</p>

<div class="emma-chips">
  <span class="emma-chip emma-chip--info" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-chip__i">🔓</span> Top stays OPEN<span class="emma-chip__detail">No chest-to-chest, no knee-on-belly, no body lock. Top camps off to the side, past the hips, leaving the space open. Closing the position is a rule break, the round resets.</span></span>
  <span class="emma-chip emma-chip--info" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-chip__i">🥊</span> Strikes scaled per level<span class="emma-chip__detail">No strikes at L1 (pure position). Light strikes from L2. Light-to-moderate ground-and-pound at L4+. Always mimic ground-and-pound, never deliver it for real.</span></span>
  <span class="emma-chip emma-chip--info" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-chip__i">⏱️</span> 20-second cap, top has no win<span class="emma-chip__detail">If top stays open-camped for 20 seconds without bottom escaping, the round resets, same roles. Top has no positive win, the clock is bottom's threat window, not top's score.</span></span>
  <span class="emma-chip emma-chip--info" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-chip__i">🦶</span> Three observable escapes<span class="emma-chip__detail">Bottom wins by standing cleanly, reversing to top, or closing a leg loop around top's head and arm. The closed loop is the structural geometry, the submission finish is not required.</span></span>
  <span class="emma-chip emma-chip--info" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-chip__i">⬛</span> Stay inside the perimeter<span class="emma-chip__detail">Play happens on mats inside a marked perimeter. If a player rolls fully out of it, that player loses the round, training the mat-edge awareness a fighter needs.</span></span>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">How to Win</p>

<div class="emma-outcomes">
  <div class="emma-outcome emma-outcome--win emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-outcome__tag">Stand</span> Bottom stands clean → bottom wins, switch roles.<span class="emma-pop">Full separation to feet, top has no connection on bottom's legs or hips. If top connects on the way up, the stand is not clean, continue.</span></div>
  <div class="emma-outcome emma-outcome--win emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-outcome__tag">Reverse</span> Bottom reverses to top → bottom wins, switch roles.<span class="emma-pop">Bottom gets above the opponent, on top of them or standing over them in control. Reversal beats damage, the position has flipped.</span></div>
  <div class="emma-outcome emma-outcome--win emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-outcome__tag">Loop</span> Bottom closes a leg loop on head and arm → bottom wins.<span class="emma-pop">Legs closed around top's head and one of top's arms, the closed structural geometry of triangle or armbar. The closed loop is the win, the finish is post-game and not required for scoring.</span></div>
  <div class="emma-outcome emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-outcome__tag">Reset</span> Top open-camps the full 20s, no escape → reset, same roles.<span class="emma-pop">Top stayed open and bottom failed all three escapes before the cap. The round resets from open camp for another rep. Top still does not score, the clock is the threat window.</span></div>
  <div class="emma-outcome emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-outcome__tag">Reset</span> Top closes the position → reset, rule break.<span class="emma-pop">If top achieves chest-to-chest, knee-on-belly, or a body lock, the open-camp constraint is broken and the round resets to the start. Top does not score from a closure, the game is not for that.</span></div>
  <div class="emma-outcome emma-outcome--lose emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-outcome__tag">Loss</span> Roll fully out of the perimeter → that player loses.<span class="emma-pop">Crossing the marked perimeter loses the round instantly, regardless of position, training the mat-edge awareness a fighter needs.</span></div>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">The Levels</p>

<div class="emma-stepper">
  <div class="emma-step emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-step__n">1</span><b>Position only</b>No strikes.<span class="emma-pop emma-pop--l">Top stays open-camped without strikes. Bottom drills the three escape paths against a positional-only top. Calibrates the open-camp shape and the closed-loop geometry.</span></div>
  <div class="emma-step emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-step__n">2</span><b>Light strikes</b>Damage threat on.<span class="emma-pop emma-pop--l">Top adds light strikes from the open camp. Bottom must defend strikes AND find one of the three escapes. The strike threat changes which escape is available moment to moment.</span></div>
  <div class="emma-step emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-step__n">3</span><b>Side switching</b>Top swaps the camp.<span class="emma-pop emma-pop--l">Top may switch sides past the hips, increasing the scramble demand on bottom. Reading which side is loaded becomes the task.</span></div>
  <div class="emma-step emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-step__n">4</span><b>Sustained ground-and-pound</b>Light-to-moderate.<span class="emma-pop emma-pop--l">Top stays open-camped and lands sustained light-to-moderate strikes, simulating real ground-and-pound. Bottom must time the escape under continuous damage threat.</span></div>
  <div class="emma-step emma-has-pop" tabindex="0"><span class="emma-step__n">5</span><b>Full expression</b>Live, open hunting.<span class="emma-pop emma-pop--l">Top stays open-camped and hunts damage with everything they have inside the rules. Bottom finds the escape under live pressure. Most representative of the real ground-and-pound problem.</span></div>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">Recall Check</p>

<div class="emma-recall">
  <p class="emma-recall__lead">Test yourself before moving on. Answer out loud, then reveal what good looks like.</p>
  <details class="emma-recall__item">
    <summary><span class="emma-recall__q">Q</span> Why is the top NOT allowed to close the position?</summary>
    <div class="emma-recall__a">The constraint <b>isolates the open-camped ground-and-pound problem</b>. Closing to chest-to-chest, knee-on-belly, or a body lock collapses it into a regular pin, a different game (mount, side, KOB). Open camp is the variable being trained here.</div>
  </details>
  <details class="emma-recall__item">
    <summary><span class="emma-recall__q">Q</span> Why does the closed loop count without finishing the submission?</summary>
    <div class="emma-recall__a">The <b>closed structural geometry</b> (legs around head-and-arm) is the observable terminal event. The finish is post-game, requires control speed and a tap, which is not what's being trained here. Closing the loop proves the escape via attack.</div>
  </details>
  <details class="emma-recall__item">
    <summary><span class="emma-recall__q">Q</span> What counts as a "clean stand," and what doesn't?</summary>
    <div class="emma-recall__a">A clean stand is <b>full separation to feet with no top connection</b>. If top connects on a leg or hip on the way up, the stand is not clean, continue. The standard is the same as a wrestling stand-up, hands and hips off, range restored.</div>
  </details>
  <details class="emma-recall__item">
    <summary><span class="emma-recall__q">Q</span> Why does the top have no win condition?</summary>
    <div class="emma-recall__a">Bottom is the <b>protagonist</b>. Top's job is to BE the threat, not to score against it. The 20-second clock is bottom's threat window, not top's score, this keeps top hunting damage rather than stalling for time.</div>
  </details>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">Go Deeper</p>

??? note "Task focus &amp; coaching cues"

    <p class="emma-sub">Each role's job</p>

    <div class="emma-cards">
      <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🤸</div><div class="emma-card__t">Bottom (Defender)</div><p>Three escape paths active at all times. Read the load, time the stand, hunt the closed loop on a committed arm, look for the reverse when top's base lightens.</p></div>
      <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🥊</div><div class="emma-card__t">Top (Open Camp)</div><p>Stay off to the side past the hips. Hunt damage with hands and feet inside the rules. Do not close the position, it breaks the game. Keep moving, side-switch as openings appear.</p></div>
    </div>

    <p class="emma-sub">Coaching cues</p>

    <div class="emma-cards">
      <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">⚓</div><div class="emma-card__t">Which path opened?</div><p>Ask bottom after each rep: "Stand, reverse, or loop?" Direct attention to which one was available, not to a memorised escape.</p></div>
      <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🔓</div><div class="emma-card__t">Did you stay open?</div><p>Ask top: "Were you off to the side, or did you slip into a pin?" Keeps the open-camp constraint honest.</p></div>
    </div>

??? abstract "Constraints-Led analysis"

    <p class="emma-sub">Constraints → Affordances</p>

    <div class="emma-flow">
      <div class="emma-flow__row"><span class="emma-flow__from">Top must stay open</span><span class="emma-flow__arr">→</span><span class="emma-flow__to">Isolates the open-camped GnP problem, not a regular pin</span></div>
      <div class="emma-flow__row"><span class="emma-flow__from">Three observable escapes</span><span class="emma-flow__arr">→</span><span class="emma-flow__to">Multiple solutions, develops degeneracy</span></div>
      <div class="emma-flow__row"><span class="emma-flow__from">Top has no win, 20s cap</span><span class="emma-flow__arr">→</span><span class="emma-flow__to">Urgency for the bottom, no stalling for the top</span></div>
      <div class="emma-flow__row"><span class="emma-flow__from">Closed loop is enough</span><span class="emma-flow__arr">→</span><span class="emma-flow__to">Observable terminal proxy, no tap required for scoring</span></div>
      <div class="emma-flow__row"><span class="emma-flow__from">Live, light-to-moderate strikes</span><span class="emma-flow__arr">→</span><span class="emma-flow__to">Keeps the strike-threat perception intact</span></div>
    </div>

    <p class="emma-hl">Implements <b>Constrain to Afford</b> (Renshaw et al., 2019). The open-camp constraint creates the affordance of escape-by-attack (closed loop) and escape-by-movement (stand, reverse), which the regular pin geometry would not allow. The closed-loop-as-win is a sharp affordance, the geometry itself is the proof.</p>

    <p class="emma-sub">What the bottom reads</p>

    <div class="emma-cards">
      <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">✋</div><div class="emma-card__t">Haptic</div><p>Load through top's hands and base → which side is committed, when an arm leaves range, when the camp lightens for a stand.</p></div>
      <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">👁️</div><div class="emma-card__t">Visual</div><p>Top's hand committing to a strike → the arm that closes the loop. Top's base on one side → the lightened side to stand toward.</p></div>
      <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🧭</div><div class="emma-card__t">Proprioceptive</div><p>Own hips and feet → whether a clean stand is set up or a connection on the leg will catch the rise.</p></div>
    </div>

    <p class="emma-sub">What we measure (order parameter)</p>

    <p class="emma-hl">Whether the bottom <b>matches the escape to the moment</b>, the stand to the lightened side, the loop to the committed arm, the reverse to the over-extended base. Track which of the three paths is taken across reps and whether each is matched to the actual opening. When the bottom consistently picks the right escape for the moment, the skill has formed.</p>

    <p class="emma-sub">Representativeness</p>

    <p class="emma-hl"><b>Models:</b> the open-camped ground-and-pound problem in MMA, where the top fighter has space to strike but cannot or will not commit to a pin, and the bottom must escape via attack or movement before damage stacks up.</p>

    <div class="emma-meta"><span>Simplified: top stays open</span><span>20s threat window</span><span>three observable escapes</span></div>

    <p class="emma-sub">Readiness to progress</p>

    <ul class="emma-checklist">
      <li>Reads the open-camp load and picks the matching escape</li>
      <li>Stands cleanly when the lightened side appears</li>
      <li>Closes the loop on a committed arm, not on a stalled one</li>
      <li>Continues escaping under sustained light-to-moderate strikes (L4)</li>
    </ul>

    <p class="emma-sub">Warning signs</p>

    <div class="emma-warns">
      <span class="emma-warn">Waits for a pin to escape from (game is open-camp only)</span>
      <span class="emma-warn">Tries to finish the submission instead of closing the loop</span>
      <span class="emma-warn">Stands without checking top's connection</span>
      <span class="emma-warn">Top drifts into chest-to-chest (rule break, reset)</span>
    </div>
