<div class="emma-meta"><span>Ground Control</span><span>Grappling</span><span>Foundational</span><span>Graded Outcome</span></div>

<p class="emma-hl"><b>Guard recovery</b> is not a single moment, it is a <b>spectrum of how much of your lower body you have reinserted between you and the opponent.</b> The more leg you get back across the line, the more secure the guard and the cleaner the escape.</p>

<div class="emma-band">
  <p class="emma-statement">Guard is not a switch, <br><span class="accent">it's a ladder.</span></p>
  <p class="emma-statement__sub">Every leg you get back between you is a rung climbed. <b>Score the rung, not a yes or no.</b></p>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">The Recovery Ladder</p>

<p>Ordered least-to-most recovered. Each rung is observable across the mat, which is what makes it a usable win condition.</p>

<div class="emma-cards">
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🦶</div><div class="emma-card__t">One foot back</div><p>The first point reinserted, the pass is stalled but no real guard yet. <b>Minimal.</b></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🦵</div><div class="emma-card__t">One knee between</div><p>Knee shield or half guard, a leg now traps the opponent. <b>The floor for a recovered guard.</b></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🧎</div><div class="emma-card__t">Both knees between</div><p>Double knee shield, both legs frame the hips. <b>Controlling.</b></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🦿</div><div class="emma-card__t">Both feet between</div><p>Feet on the hips or hooks in, you own the distance. <b>Full open guard.</b></p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🔒</div><div class="emma-card__t">Closed loop</div><p>Legs wrapped and locked behind the opponent, closing the guard. <b>Most secure.</b></p></div>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">It Mirrors Passing</p>

<p>The passer and the guard player work the same three lines in opposite directions. The passer wins by getting <b>past</b> the hip, knee, and foot lines; the guard player wins by reinserting <b>across</b> them. Defining recovery this way makes the two win conditions two ends of one ruler.</p>

<div class="emma-flow">
  <div class="emma-flow__row"><span class="emma-flow__from">Passer</span><span class="emma-flow__arr">→</span><span class="emma-flow__to">Past the line of feet → past the knees → past the hips → pin</span></div>
  <div class="emma-flow__row"><span class="emma-flow__from">Guard player</span><span class="emma-flow__arr">←</span><span class="emma-flow__to">Reinsert across the hips → the knees → the feet → closed loop</span></div>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">How We Score It</p>

<p>Recovery is a <b>graded win</b>, not pass or fail. A game sets a <b>floor</b> (the minimum that counts as escaped) and then rewards getting further up the ladder.</p>

<div class="emma-flow">
  <div class="emma-flow__row"><span class="emma-flow__from">Floor</span><span class="emma-flow__arr">→</span><span class="emma-flow__to">One knee in (half guard), the realistic recovery from a flattened position</span></div>
  <div class="emma-flow__row"><span class="emma-flow__from">Cleaner</span><span class="emma-flow__arr">→</span><span class="emma-flow__to">Both knees → both feet → closed loop</span></div>
  <div class="emma-flow__row"><span class="emma-flow__from">Full escape</span><span class="emma-flow__arr">→</span><span class="emma-flow__to">A reversal to top, or standing up, out entirely</span></div>
</div>

<p class="emma-hl">A game may set its floor lower (one foot back, for beginners) or higher (full guard only) to match the position it trains. From <a href="../../games/mount-escape/">mount</a>, the honest floor is half guard, you rarely recover straight to a closed guard.</p>

<p class="emma-glabel">Common Errors</p>

<div class="emma-cards">
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">⏸️</div><div class="emma-card__t">Surviving, not recovering</div><p>Holding still under pressure feels safe but climbs no rung. Recovery means reinserting a leg, not waiting.</p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">🪜</div><div class="emma-card__t">Skipping rungs</div><p>Reaching for closed guard before a knee is even in. Climb the ladder in order, each rung makes the next reachable.</p></div>
  <div class="emma-card"><div class="emma-card__i">❎</div><div class="emma-card__t">Calling it binary</div><p>Treating "guard or no guard" loses the feedback. Half guard recovered is real progress and should score as such.</p></div>
</div>

<p class="emma-glabel">Developed Through Games</p>

<div class="emma-flow">
  <div class="emma-flow__row"><span class="emma-flow__from"><a href="../../games/mount-escape/">Mount Escape</a></span><span class="emma-flow__arr">→</span><span class="emma-flow__to">Recover from under mount, floor at half guard</span></div>
  <div class="emma-flow__row"><span class="emma-flow__from"><a href="../../games/ground-escape/">Ground Escape</a></span><span class="emma-flow__arr">→</span><span class="emma-flow__to">Recover across the major pinning positions</span></div>

</div>

<p class="emma-hl">The fighter who recovers guard does not just survive, they <b>climb back into options</b>. Each rung returns a little more control over the distance and the next exchange.</p>
