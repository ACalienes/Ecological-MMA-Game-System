The Premise

Games,
Not Drills

Instead of repeating fixed movements in isolation, athletes solve problems through structured games. Difficulty self-regulates, solutions are discovered, and every rep is representative of real fighting.

Every game ultimately leads to one of three endings — knockout, TKO, or submission. Finish the fight while taking as little damage as possible.

“The environment does the teaching. Correct behavior emerges from constraints, not coaching cues.”
