\# Concept: TKO Pin

\#\# Definition

A \*\*TKO Pin\*\* is a control state where:  
\- hips and shoulders are meaningfully controlled  
\- posture and mobility are compromised  
\- \*\*at least one limb is free to strike effectively\*\*  
\- defender options are high-cost

TKO Pin is a \*\*state\*\*, not a position.

\---

\#\# Properties

\- often fleeting  
\- sometimes stabilizable  
\- exists on wall or ground  
\- trades control for strike access

\---

\#\# Quality Spectrum

\- Threat  
\- Stable  
\- Dominant

\---

\#\# Training Focus

Create → Capture → Stabilize → Re-acquire

\---

TKO Pin definition may evolve with system refinement.
